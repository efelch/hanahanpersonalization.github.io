About Us

Welcome to Hanahan Personalization! We are Edward and Monica Felch, and we create custom, personalized gifts and decor for important moments. We make everything in our workshop, which includes a wide variety of woodworking tools as well as laser engravers.

What started as a passion for creating meaningful gifts and decorations has grown into our small business where we bring your visions to life. Let us know what you want, and we can make it happen!

We are local first - We believe in supporting local businesses and communities, and we strive to make a positive impact in our community.
